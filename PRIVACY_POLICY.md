# Privacy Policy for PostPlanner

**Last updated: December 2, 2024**

## Introduction

PostPlanner ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Account Information**: Email address and password when you register
- **Client Data**: Client names and information you add to manage your social media clients
- **Post Content**: Text, images, and scheduling information for social media posts you create
- **Notes**: Any notes you create for your clients

### Automatically Collected Information
- **Device Information**: We may collect device type and operating system version
- **Usage Data**: Information about how you use the app

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:
- Provide and maintain the PostPlanner service
- Allow you to create and manage social media posts
- Store your client information and scheduling data
- Sync your data across devices

## Permissions

### Camera Permission
We request camera permission to allow you to:
- Take photos for social media posts
- Upload images to your post content

**Note**: We only access your camera when you explicitly choose to take a photo. We do not access your camera in the background or without your permission.

### Storage Permission
We request storage permission to:
- Allow you to select images from your device gallery
- Save post content locally

## Data Storage

- Your data is stored securely on our servers
- We use MongoDB Atlas for database hosting
- Images are stored on Cloudinary (if you upload any)
- We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data

## Data Sharing

We do NOT:
- Sell your personal information to third parties
- Share your data with advertisers
- Use your data for marketing purposes

We may share data only:
- If required by law
- To protect our rights or the rights of others

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your personal data
- Delete your account and all associated data
- Update or correct your information
- Export your data

To exercise these rights, contact us at the email below.

## Children's Privacy

PostPlanner is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy.

## Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:
- **MongoDB Atlas**: Database hosting
- **Cloudinary**: Image storage (optional)
- **Render**: Backend hosting

These services have their own privacy policies governing the use of your information.

## Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, we will delete all your data within 30 days.
